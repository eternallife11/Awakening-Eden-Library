# Awakening Eden Brand Bible v1.0
*A living design system for regeneration, holistic living, learning and growing together.*

## The Core Feeling
Awakening Eden should feel like arriving at a beautiful living home: calm, earthy, generous, human, sacred and silly. It is not only a website. It is a place of regenerative interbeing, empowerment, belonging, memory and movement.

When someone leaves Awakening Eden, they should feel:
- calmer than when they arrived
- more hopeful than when they arrived
- more connected than when they arrived
- more capable of taking one small step

## The Promise
**Leave life more alive.**

A more beautiful world is not something we wait for. It is something we grow — within ourselves, with one another, and together with the living Earth, to thrive as one family of life.

## The Voice
Speak like a trusted friend walking beside the reader through a garden.

Warm. Grounded. Poetic when useful. Practical always. Never overwhelming. Never fear-based. Never performative. Honest, human, hopeful and humble.

Use phrases like:
- Welcome home.
- Dear fellow gardener, lover, guardian...
- Come walk with us.
- Start where you are.
- One seed at a time.
- Take what nourishes you. Leave something beautiful behind.
- Sacred and silly.
- Perfectly imperfect.
- Leave every place a little more alive than you found it.

## Design Principles
1. Nature is the greatest designer.
2. Less, but better.
3. Beauty slows people down.
4. Hope is practical.
5. Small actions ripple.
6. Learning should feel joyful.
7. People remember feelings.
8. Community is part of the curriculum.
9. Leave room for wonder.
10. Leave life more alive.

## Visual Identity
The website should feel like an old botanical field journal lovingly kept over many years, blended with a premium regenerative retreat welcome book.

### Colours
- Deep forest green: #102819
- Forest: #173822
- Leaf green: #365c3b
- Moss: #6d8b5b
- Sage: #b9c8a7
- Terracotta: #b56a42
- Clay: #d8b9a3
- Warm sand: #f6f2ea
- Cream paper: #fffdf7
- Charcoal ink: #263326

### Typography
Use an elegant serif feeling for main titles, highly readable sans-serif body text, and a handwritten font only for intimate notes.

Recommended hierarchy:
- Big serif titles for grounding and beauty
- Clean body text for readability
- Handwritten notes for warmth and memory

### Illustration Style
Use subtle hand-drawn botanical details:
- olive branches
- fern leaves
- seed with roots
- sprouting plant
- butterfly
- bee
- mushroom
- rosemary, lavender, fig leaf
- small vines and dividers
- tiny hearts and handwritten notes

All should feel like fine brown ink with delicate watercolor washes on warm paper.

4. Begin Your Journey

6. Your Awakening & Syntropy Journey

10. Our hope for your journey
11. Footer blessing

## Guide System
Every guide should follow:
Welcome → Why it matters → Simple understanding → Practical steps → Benjy’s field wisdom → Sofia’s reflection when relevant → Recap → One action today → Join community → Gift back.

## Content Rule
Every page should answer:
- Where am I?
- What is this?
- Why should I care?
- Where do I begin?
- What is one next step?

## Photography Style
Real life over stock images. Golden hour. Hands in soil. Tea. Books. Dogs. Laughter. Bare feet. Rain. Mud. Community. Campfires. Food forests. Seedlings. Yoga outdoors. Ocean. Mountains. Children. Elders. Shared meals.

## Business Style
Income should grow from service and value:
- gift economy
- beautiful guides
- regenerative consultations
- workshops
- courses
- retreats
- property design
- community memberships
- healing and regeneration centre vision

Never manipulate. Never use fear as a sales tool. Create value first. Receive gratefully.

## Final Blessing

May the gardens you create nourish others.
May your unique gifts find beautiful ways to serve life.
And may we leave this Earth, together, a little more alive than we found it.
