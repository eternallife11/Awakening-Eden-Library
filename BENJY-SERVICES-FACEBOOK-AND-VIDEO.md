## The clearest offer architecture

Lead with three ways to begin instead of asking people to choose between many technical services:

1. **Online Regenerative Clarity Session** — from scattered ideas to a grounded 30–90 day direction.
2. **On-site Property Walk & 90-Day Action Map** — read the land together and organise what comes first.
3. **Living Land Regeneration Partnership** — concept design, implementation support, team learning and seasonal adaptation.

Specialist capabilities can then be woven around each property: tired-orchard revival, water resilience, living soil, food forests, syntropic agroforestry, Abundant Edge biomass systems, project support and workshops.

The core promise:

> From property overwhelm to a living plan—and from tired land to a system that becomes more resilient, abundant and understood each season.

## 60–75 second presentation video

### Simple filming plan

- Film vertically for Facebook and Instagram; keep your eyes near the top third of the frame.
- Stand outdoors with a real orchard, garden or living landscape behind you.
- Use soft morning or late-afternoon light and keep the sound clear.
- Speak to one person who loves their land but feels unsure where to begin.
- Add short cutaways: dry or tired soil, rainfall or water pathways, prunings and mulch, living roots, a food forest, you teaching, people planting.
- End on your face and show the tracked website link for at least three seconds.

Hi, I’m Benjy from Awakening Eden. I help people move from property overwhelm—and tired orchards or land—to a clear regenerative pathway.
