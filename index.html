A place to return to

A home for practical hope.

Awakening Eden is a living library, a gathering place and an invitation to remember what becomes possible when knowledge meets love—and love becomes action.

We are not waiting for a more beautiful world to arrive. We are learning to become its gardeners, listeners, builders and joyful protectors.

Here, regeneration is both inner and outer: healthy soil and steadier nervous systems; water held in the land and wisdom carried through community; food forests, holistic living and the courage to begin where we are.

Come for a guide, a garden plan, an inspiring film or a quiet breath. Leave with one living next step—and return whenever you need.

The future is something we practise together.

The gardens that grew us

We are Benjy & Sofia

Yogis, Earth lovers, regenerative educators and lifelong students of life.

Across seven formative years in Australia, gardens, teachers, circles and communities changed the way we listened—to the land, to one another and to the deeper relationships that sustain life.

Through more than twenty-five retreats and circles, we have learnt that healing the Earth and healing ourselves are not separate journeys. Awakening Eden is our imperfectly perfect answer to the question: What is ours to do?

Australia - learning to listen

Gratitude carried forward

For seven formative years, Australia changed the way we listened—to the land, to one another and to the deeper relationships that sustain life. We carry gratitude for the Country and communities that held this chapter of our journey.

Awakening Eden does not claim First Nations stories or symbols as its own. We honour living cultures with humility and believe true relationship begins with listening, respect, reciprocity and care.

With gratitude Benjy remembers Gabu Damien, a Warungu man from Far North Queensland, and the gardens and lessons in listening they created together. We thank Adam Collins, and Kate and Stu, our biodynamic teachers and wisdom keepers. And we hold special gratitude for Dan, Grace and Mark of the Sacred Earth Foundation family, whose vision and ours grew into a joined mission.
