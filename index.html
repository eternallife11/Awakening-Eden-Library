Dear fellow gardener, lover, guardian...

We are Benjy & Sofia.

Earth lovers. Regenerative educators. Permies. Yogis. Ocean lovers. Guardians and stewards of life. Community builders. Lifelong students of nature. Dreamers. Doers. Always learning. Always evolving. Perfectly imperfect.

United by a shared love for life, and a belief that together we can leave the world a little more alive than we found it — with every step we take, every hug we share, every seed we plant, every tree we nurture, every authentic gathering we hold, and every act of care offered to each other and all life.

Read why this became our life’s work

Benjy’s heart mission has long been to gather the sparks that helped us wake up — little changes of thought, behaviour, emotion, hope and faith — and turn them into a Living Library for positive change. A place where inspiration becomes practice, practice becomes confidence, and confidence becomes care in action.

Over time, it became one huge regenerative, spiritual hero’s journey — grounded in soil, service, healing, learning, humility and love. Awakening Eden is our antidote to despair and hopelessness: a living invitation to remember that solutions exist, beauty is still here, and every caring action can become part of the regeneration of life.

This is part of our mission and our life’s work. All our journeys, all our questions, all our story became our offering because we wanted to see solutions. We asked for guidance. We asked: what is our place, what are we meant to do, why is all this happening, and what are the life-giving ways forward?

Welcome. We are so glad you found your way here.

Whether you are here to plant your first herb, learn about regeneration, watch a living library of films and documentaries for action and remembering, discover books and inspiring teachers, build a thriving food forest, deepen your yoga or holistic practice, reconnect with nature, or simply remember that another way of living is not only possible — but already growing — you are warmly welcome.

Many of us feel that another way of living is both possible and deeply needed. Our hope is that this library helps people discover practical, beautiful ways to contribute to that future — one seed, one breath, one guide, one film, one gathering, one act of care at a time.

This Living Library is our first gift to the world: a home for regeneration, awakening, empowerment, calm, peace, purpose, love, belonging, remembering and joyful movement.

It is also our response to the ache so many people feel when they look at the world. Instead of collapsing into despair, we chose to gather hope, practices, teachers, land-based wisdom, stories and solutions into one place people can return to when they need courage and direction.

For many years, Benjy dreamed of creating a library for positive change: one place to gather the films, books, teachers, practices, regenerative wisdom and lived experiences that changed our lives, helped us find direction, and inspired us to serve life more deeply.

Now, together with Sofia, this dream is becoming a living home — a place to begin, return, learn, share, laugh, reflect and grow together.

Regenerative interbeing.

Our Philosophy

Many traditions and disciplines that inspired us point toward a similar understanding: life flourishes through relationship.

Yoga invites us to cultivate awareness and compassion. Ecology reveals living relationships. Regenerative agriculture restores the health of whole systems. Permaculture encourages us to observe, care and design with nature. Syntropic farming demonstrates how diversity, succession and cooperation can create increasing abundance over time.

Together, these perspectives inspire what we call regenerative interbeing: living in ways that help ourselves, our communities and the living world flourish together.

These values are not rules. They are intentions we return to again and again: care for all life, question our actions, reflect together, improve what we can without burning out, nourish ourselves while nourishing the world, and choose kindness, compassion, peace and aligned action when needed.

Our Hope

We do not believe one guide will change the world by itself.

But perhaps one idea, one conversation, one seed, one restored patch of soil, one act of kindness, one shared meal, one brave decision, and one moment of remembering can begin a quiet ripple that reaches farther than we can see.

Our hope is that Awakening Eden helps create more of those ripples — helping people feel more hopeful, more connected, and more capable of caring for themselves, for each other, and for the living Earth.
