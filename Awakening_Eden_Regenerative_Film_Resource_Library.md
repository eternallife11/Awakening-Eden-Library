## BOOKS: bibles for regeneration, soil health, ecosystems, permaculture and syntropy
- **Dirt to Soil** - Gabe Brown. Soil armor, living roots, diversity, animals and regenerative farmer courage.
- **Restoration Agriculture** - Mark Shepard. Perennial agriculture, tree crops, polycultures and ecosystem mimicry.
- **Gaia's Garden** - Toby Hemenway. Home-scale permaculture guilds, abundance and ecological gardening.
- **Edible Forest Gardens** - Dave Jacke & Eric Toensmeier. Deep food-forest ecology, succession, layers and design process.
- **Earth Restorer's Guide to Permaculture** - Rosemary Morrow. Practical permaculture design for restoring land, communities and resilience.
- **Permaculture: Principles & Pathways** - David Holmgren. Design principles for living systems, resilience and regenerative culture.
- **Permaculture: A Designers Manual** - Bill Mollison. The big permaculture bible: zones, sectors, water, trees, buildings and communities.
- **Soil, Not Oil** - Vandana Shiva. Food, climate, seed freedom and ecological agriculture.
- **For the Love of Soil** - Nicole Masters. Soil observation, minerals, biology and living fertility.
- **Braiding Sweetgrass** - Robin Wall Kimmerer. Indigenous wisdom, botany and gratitude woven together.
- **The One-Straw Revolution** - Masanobu Fukuoka. Natural farming and working with nature.
- **Creating a Forest Garden** - Martin Crawford. Temperate food-forest layers, species, guilds and practical abundance.
- **Finding the Mother Tree** - Suzanne Simard. Forest communication, care and the hidden life of trees.
- **The More Beautiful World Our Hearts Know Is Possible** - Charles Eisenstein. Interbeing, culture shift and acting from the new story.
- **Active Hope** - Joanna Macy & Chris Johnstone. Transform ecological grief and overwhelm into grounded action.
